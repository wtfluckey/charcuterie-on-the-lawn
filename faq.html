Frequently Asked Questions

Is this event free?

YES!

What is provided?

A large and diverse selection of cured meats, pates, and terrines will be provided by Shawn Bennigsdorf and Randy Brown. We will also have home brewed beer provided by Ron DeGeest.

Is this event outside?

Yep! The event is located at beautiful Foxhollow Poultry Farm and will be happening rain or shine. Be sure to check the weather the morning of the event.

What should I bring?

Feel free to bring coolers and any beverages of your own, lawn chairs and blankets, as well as sunscreen or ponchos, depending on the weather.

Where should I park?

We encourage carpooling as parking is slightly limited. Park anywhere on the side of NE 56th St.

Will there be entertainment?

We have all kinds of entertainment! Chris Falcon will be playing the guitar and Dante Powell will be doing comedy.

Can kids come?

This event is tailored to adults, but kids are absolutely welcome.

What about dogs?

While we love our furry friends, they tend to get a little excited around lots of people and great meat, so we kindly ask that you leave your pups at home.
