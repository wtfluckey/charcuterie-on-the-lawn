Chris Falcon

Music

After playing in the Baltimore music scene for years untold in a variety of bands, Chris Falcon has taken wing on his own and brought his brand of folky.acoustic.rock. to Des Moines. Blending folk and bluegrass structures, impassioned smokey vocals and feet planted firmly on the ground, Chris connects with his audience on an everyday “walking around the neighborhood” level. As he finishes production on his forthcoming record, you can catch one of his high energy live shows in and around Des Moines.
